<p>unexpands <b>tab</b> to <b>spaces</b>, <code>-t</code> is required to specify number of spaces to unexpand.</p>

<pre class="language-bash command-line" data-user="user" data-host="milsetone" data-output="2">
	<code>
		echo "	a sample string" | unexpand -t 4
		    a sample string
	</code>
</pre>
