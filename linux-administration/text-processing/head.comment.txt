<p>Prints the first 10 lines of a file.</p>
<p>If option <code>-n N</code> is given, first <code>N</code> lines of text is printed.</p>

<pre class="language-bash command-line" data-user="user" data-host="milestone" data-output="2,3">
	<code>
		head -n 2 /etc/os-release
		NAME="Arch Linux"
		PRETTY_NAME="Arch Linux"
	</code>
</pre>
